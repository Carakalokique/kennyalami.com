Dollar A Day

The idea is simple. Sell minimum $1 worth of stuff every day for 100 days. I started on Friday, June 14th. As of today (July 1st), it's been 18 days.

What is it about?

I've been thinking for a while I needed to get better at selling. It's a crucial skill and I can't get around it. $1 felt achievable, a stretch but achievable. My hope is to learn a ton, overcome some emotional and mental blocks, and make a buck ;)
