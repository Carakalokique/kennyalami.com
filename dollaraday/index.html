Dollar A Day

The idea was simple. Sell minimum $1 worth of stuff every day for 100 days. I started on Friday, June 14, 2024 and lasted until July 9, 2024. I decided to stop for a variety of reasons (read more here).

What is it about?

I was thinking for a while I needed to be better at selling. It's a crucial skill and I couldn't get around it. $1 felt achievable, a stretch but achievable. My hope was to learn a ton, overcome some emotional and mental blocks, and make a buck ;)
